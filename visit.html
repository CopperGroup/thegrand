Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 30 minutes before curtain time to allow for parking, picking up tickets, and finding your seat.

Is there a dress code?

While there is no strict dress code, we encourage smart casual attire to enhance the theatre experience.

Can I take photos during the show?

Photography and recording are not permitted during performances to respect the artists and other patrons.

Are children allowed?

Children are welcome at age-appropriate performances. Please check individual show listings for age recommendations.
