A legacy of artistic excellence

For over 75 years, The Grand Theatre has been the cultural heart of our community, bringing world-class performances to audiences who appreciate the transformative power of live theatre.

Our intimate 500-seat venue creates an unparalleled connection between performers and audience, making every show a truly immersive experience.
